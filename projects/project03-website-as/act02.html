Notions of home begin to exist in a void for imagination to fill. It holds fragments of stories untold by time, places lost in memory, and histories erased through politics. Return, becomes a political act that is practiced at present, and projected as an image into an uncertain future. The once displayed, destroyed, or forgotten becomes a story of existence, of permanence and of belonging.
